About Custom Roadmaps

Custom roadmaps allow you to create personalized learning paths for any topic or skill.

How it works:
  1. Create a roadmap with basic information
  2. Add learning nodes with content and resources
  3. Arrange nodes in the desired order
  4. Optionally share your roadmap with others

After creating your roadmap, you'll be able to add learning nodes and resources to build out your complete learning path.

Tips

  • Be specific about the skills or knowledge your roadmap covers
  • Choose an appropriate difficulty level to set expectations
  • Use relevant tags to make your roadmap discoverable
  • Consider making your roadmap public to help others learn
