About Cloning

Cloning creates a copy of the original roadmap that you can customize to fit your specific needs and learning style.

What gets cloned:
  • All learning nodes and their content
  • All resource links
  • The overall structure and organization

After cloning, you'll be able to:

  • Add, edit, or remove nodes
  • Reorder the learning sequence
  • Add your own resource links
  • Customize all content to your preferences

Tips

  • Consider adding your name or a personal identifier to the title
  • Update the description to reflect your customizations
  • Adjust the difficulty level if you plan to simplify or expand the content
  • Add tags that reflect your specific focus or interests
